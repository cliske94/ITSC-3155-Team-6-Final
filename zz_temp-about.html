Our Story

We started Pet Pals as group project for our Software Engineering class at The University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Pet Pals demonstrates the skills we learned during the Spring 2022 semester. As an agile software engineering team, we created Pet Pals to be a SaaS (Software as a Service) web application. We hope your enjoy your visit!

What is Pet Pals?

Pet Pals is a forum-based application where users can create accounts, user profiles, and create posts. Our vision is that our users will connect with other pet owners to create lasting connections and friendships. Please visit our FAQs page for more information.
